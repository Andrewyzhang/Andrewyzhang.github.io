Extracurricular activities

Outside of academic courses, I keep myself busy through a variety of activities. One word that influences my life the most: strings.

  • Music! As a young first grader who had no concept of free will, I was granted the chance to play a fancy wooden box with horsehair on a stick. From that faithful moment, I would call myself a violinist. While solo performances scare me stiff, the community I found in orchestras and chamber groups foster my love for classical repertoire. On the road, I taught myself viola, which exposed me to a whole new perspective in ensemble music (and also viola jokes).
  • Tennis! Something that my entire family participates in. Alongside ice hockey and swimming, it has kept me actively on my feet and has been a stress reliever throughout my academic years. My Saturday mornings have been defined by heading out to the courts and playing friendly matches with family and friends, and the community that formed around me from that shared passion is one I will always cherish.
